Frequently Asked Questions

What is Janjagurati ?
Janjagurati is an e-Marketplace for Govt. schemes and services. Using Janjagurati you can find various government schemes, check your eligibility, and apply for the schemes.
How Janjagurati will help common citizens?
Janjagurati will reduce the time and effort of citizens in searching for appropriate government schemes.The easy user interface of Janjagurati helps common people a lot in discovering and applying for schemes.
Can I apply for the schemes through Janjagurati?
For now, the platform shall direct you to the application page of the scheme of your choice.It is envisaged that in the upcoming phases, Janjagurati shall have the feature to apply for schemes / services from within the platform/ app.
How does Janjagurati work? How do I check for my eligibility through Janjagurati ?
Janjagurati offers a convenient three - step process for the citizens to discover the government schemes for which they are eligible: Step 1 - The user enters his / her attributes such as demographic, income, social details, etc.Step 2 - The Janjagurati findsthe relevant schemes from hundreds of schemes for the user.Step 3 - The user can select from the list of eligible schemes and get more information from the dedicated scheme page with detailed knowledge, FAQs, and application process and traverse to the application URL to apply.
